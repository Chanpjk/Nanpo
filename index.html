⬇️ Download Nanpo for macOS

After downloading, follow the installation guide to allow the app through security settings.

Installation Guide

  1. Click the Download button above and save NanpoApp.zip to your Mac.
  2. Unzip the archive and drag Nanpo.app into your Applications folder.
  3. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down to the “Security” section; you should see a message about Nanpo being blocked. Click Allow Anyway.
  5. Open the Nanpo app again. macOS will ask for your password or fingerprint – authenticate to proceed.
  6. You’re all set! Explore the tweaks and customize your Mac.
